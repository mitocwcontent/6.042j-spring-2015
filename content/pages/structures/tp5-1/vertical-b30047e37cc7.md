                \\=GCD(874, 196)=GCD(196, rem(874,196))
                \\=GCD(196, 90)=GCD(90, rem(196,90))
                \\=GCD(90, 16)=GCD(16, rem(90,16))
                \\=GCD(16, 10)=GCD(10, rem(16,10))
                \\=GCD(10, 6)=GCD(6, rem(10,6))
                \\=GCD(6, 4)=GCD(4, rem(6,4))
                \\=GCD(4, 2)=GCD(2, rem(4,2))
                \\=GCD(2, 0)=2\)
